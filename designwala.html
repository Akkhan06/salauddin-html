frequently asked questions

What is UBots ?

UBots is a collection of 9999 Universal Bots. They are on the Ethereum blockchain. They are designed with bright colors representing the bright future of NFTs. With over 200 different traits and accessories, UBots come in unique display.

What are they good for ?

UBots entitle the holder to grand prizes. Also, access to whitelist of near future projects.

How does the draw take place?

All holders enter the draw. The draw is random and automated. The draw is per NFT and not per holder; meaning if a holder owns more than one NFT, he or she is entitled to more spots in the draw, as per the number of NFTs he or she owns during the time of the draw. (The more NFTs you own, the more prizes you can get)

How will prizes be delivered?

Prizes will be given as worth of ETH, they will be transfered to holders' wallets. The process will take one week after the draw is made.

How to mint?

The mint portal will open in February. The price is 0.05 ETH. There is no limit for mint per wallet.
